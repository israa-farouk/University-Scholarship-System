Nominate a Student

To nominate students for a scholarship, ensure you have completed a nomination application. This includes:

  • Obtaining the consent of the student who you are nominating.
  • A clear and reasonable 200-300 word rationale for the nomination.
  • Any supporting documents or reference letters that might be of relevance.

Nominator Criteria

To qualify as a nominator, you must:

  • Be a current faculty member within good standing at the university
  • Not be a close family member, relative or friend of the nominated student
  • Have a valid @emerginguniversity.ca email address
