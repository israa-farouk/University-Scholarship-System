Emerging Scholarship

Donor - Government of Alberta

Award Description

This award is offered annually to all students in any faculty by the government of Alberta in order to financially aid students who meet the eligibility criteria. A cheque will be sent to the eligible students that applied by October 31st. Detailed information given below.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Student type: Undergraduate
  • Citizenship: Domestic
  • Faculty: Any
  • Year Enterning: 2nd3rd4th
  • The applicants should meet the following requirements:
  • Must be Canadian citizens or Permanent residents, and Alberta residents
  • Academic Merit. Must have achieved GPA of 3.20 on all courses completed in the previous fall and winter terms.
  • Minimum of 24 units or 8 courses completed in the previous fall and winter term.

Benefits

  • Award value: $1000
  • $200 bonus for students with GPA over 3.7

Deadline to apply

  • Fall: October 31st
  • Students that miss the deadline need to contact the university.

Continuing Student Scholarship

Donor - The Bajwa University

Award Description

This award is offered annually only to the continuing students in the below mentioned faculties by the Bajwa University. A cheque will be sent to the eligible students that applied by October 31st. Detailed information given below.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Student type: Undergraduate
  • Citizenship: Domestic or International
  • Faculty: Computer Science
  • Year Enterning: 2nd3rd4th
  • The applicants should meet the following requirements:
  • Must be Canadian citizens, Permanent residents or International Student residing in Alberta
  • Academic Merit. Must have achieved GPA of 3.7 on all courses completed in the previous fall and winter terms.
  • Minimum of 18 units or 6 courses completed in the previous fall and winter term.

Benefits

  • Award value: $2000
  • $800 bonus for students with GPA of 4.0

Deadline to apply

  • Fall: October 31st
  • Students that miss the deadline need to contact the university.
